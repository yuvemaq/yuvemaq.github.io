About Me

Enterprise Business Intelligence Analyst II

One of the main reasons why I pursued data analytics was because of its ever-changing, fast-pace, and stimulating ecosystem and the required caliber for an analyst to survive and succeed in such an ecosystem. I started my journey back in 2021 when I entered an online bootcamp for data science - it was lifechanging. It's funny to think about how it all started, and how long it took me to actually do it. Now, every time my mind tells me to learn a new skill, I plant to do it as soon as possible (written in a small notebook with many bullets, arrows, and ETC). Currently working as an enterprise business intelligence analyst, I am the bridge that connects business stakeholders and informatics/IT team. It's very engaging and oftentimes asking the right questions goes a long way. Efficiency is one of my top (if not, the top) priorities when I work, and so learning how to use new tools and designing good data pipelines is a current interest of mine. On a non-data and non-career related subject, some of my hobbies include running, interior designing, web designing, and music composition.
